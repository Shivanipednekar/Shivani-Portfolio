About Me

Hi, I’m Shivani. I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast with a curious mind, a love for travel, and a taste for life’s simple pleasures.

Traveling is my way of seeing the world beyond the ordinary. I explore hidden places, capturing the moments that often go unnoticed.

I express my journey through poetry, turning experiences into words that capture the spirit of each place. My notebooks are filled with lines inspired by mountains, sunsets, and bustling streets.

And when I’m not traveling or writing, you’ll find me sampling flavors from every corner, restaurant-hopping in search of dishes that tell a story.
